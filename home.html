BigBoardsHEX

Your Hex is a portable cluster that sits on your desk! But beware: its sleek design hides a full-blown data center - right at your fingertips! Fully pre-assembled by us, so you can concentrate not on the hardware but on your data.

Your Hex is by far the most productive learning and development platform for Big Data. Getting started is as easy as 1-2-3 ...

1. Connect power and network

To boot your Hex, you have to plug it to a wall outlet and hook it up to your network to provide access from your computer.

2. Browse to integrated tools

Our integrated tools help you to monitor your Hex and completely administer your device from our dashboards!

3. Install any big data tint from our library

Tints are pre-packaged technologies, open datasets or tutorials. By the click of a botton in our dashboard, tints are downloaded, installed and configured within minutes. That means you can just work on your data!
